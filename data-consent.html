Data & Privacy Consent

Before we begin, we need your informed consent to collect and use your personal and biometric data as part of the Royal Regulation program. Please read each section carefully and confirm your agreement below.

What we collect
Data We Work With
During the program we collect and work with:
  • Biometric data from your wearable device — heart rate, HRV, sleep data, stress scores, body temperature
  • Assessment scores — PSS, MAIA, and BOLT results
  • Session notes and observations from intake and weekly calls
  • Personal information you share during the program
How we use it
Purpose of Data Collection
Your data is used exclusively to:
  • Generate your personalised weekly reports
  • Track your progress and calibrate the program to your nervous system
  • Inform session content and coaching recommendations
  • Identify patterns that support your transformation
Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for any commercial purpose.
Storage & security
How Your Data is Stored
Your data is stored securely in a private Notion workspace accessible only to your coach. It is processed using encrypted tools including Make.com and the Anthropic Claude API. No data is stored on unsecured personal devices.
Your rights
Control Over Your Data
You have the right to:
  • Access a copy of all data held about you at any time
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request permanent deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time without penalty
